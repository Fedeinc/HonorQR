Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a memorial page?

To create a memorial page, navigate to the "Create Memorial" page from your dashboard. Fill in the required details, upload photos, videos, and other memories, and click "Create Memorial."

Can I edit a memorial page after it's created?

Yes, you can edit the memorial page at any time by accessing it through your profile and selecting the "Edit" option.

How do I share a memorial page with others?

Once a memorial page is created, you can share the unique URL with others or use the integrated social media sharing options available on the page.
